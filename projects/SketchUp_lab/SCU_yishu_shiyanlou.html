Experimental building Design
Art College, Sichuan University

This project showcases not only technical proficiency in design tools like AutoCAD and SketchUp but also an artistic finesse in rendering with Lumion.

    Overview:

    Embark on a virtual exploration of our intricate laboratory design project, a journey that seamlessly integrates diverse design tools to bring forth a visually captivating space. From on-site analysis and measurement to AutoCAD designing, SketchUp's 3D construction, and Lumion's transformative rendering, this project is a testament to the convergence of technology and creativity in architectural design.

    Task Breakdown:

    1. Site Analysis:

      2. Conducted detailed on-site visits to gather essential data.

      Analyzed spatial constraints and measurements, lighting conditions, and architectural nuances to inform the design process.

    2. AutoCAD Precision:

      3. Utilized AutoCAD for meticulous two-dimensional design.

      Incorporated precise measurements and architectural details to lay the foundation for the laboratory space. This was done to facilitate the #D drawing and ensure acuray.

    3. Integration Process:

      4. Implemented a seamless workflow, exporting the refined AutoCAD design into SketchUp for enhanced 3D construction capabilities.

      Ensured a smooth transition between platforms, preserving design integrity throughout the process.

    4. SketchUp 3D Construction:

      5. Transformed the AutoCAD design into an immersive three-dimensional environment using SketchUp.

      Explored spatial dynamics, ensuring optimal functionality and aesthetic appeal in the virtual realm.

    5. Lumion Rendering Magic:

      6. Elevated the design to new heights with Lumion's advanced rendering capabilities.

      Exported the intricate 3D model from SketchUp to Lumion for lifelike visualization, capturing nuanced details, lighting effects, and material textures.

    Achievements and Innovations:

    Immersive Exploration: The transition from AutoCAD to SketchUp provides stakeholders with an immersive exploration of the laboratory, fostering a deeper understanding of spatial dynamics.

    Realism Through Lumion: Lumion's rendering prowess adds a layer of realism, enabling stakeholders to envision the laboratory in its final form. This includes the play of light, shadow effects, and material finishes.

    Efficiency and Precision: The use of AutoCAD ensures meticulous precision in the foundational design, while SketchUp allows for dynamic and efficient 3D construction.

    Conclusion:

    This laboratory design project is a fusion of on-site analysis, technical precision in AutoCAD, creative exploration in SketchUp, and the transformative magic of Lumion.
